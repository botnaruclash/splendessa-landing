Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Splendessa ("we," "us") is a web design studio based in Chișinău, Republic of Moldova, building booking-first landing pages and maintenance for med spas and aesthetic clinics. This policy explains what personal data we collect through splendessa.com, why we collect it, who helps us process it, and the choices you have. We collect only what we need to reply to you and schedule a call — nothing more.

1. What we collect

We collect personal data only when you choose to give it to us:

  • Contact form. When you leave your details, we collect the clinic name, a phone or WhatsApp number, and an email address you enter.
  • Booking a call. When you book an introductory call, our scheduling provider (Cal.com) collects the name, email, and scheduling details you submit to arrange the meeting.

We do not run advertising trackers, and we do not ask for payment details on the Site. A hidden anti-spam field in the form is used only to detect automated bots; it is never shown to you and captures no personal information.

2. How we use it

We use the data you provide solely to:

  • respond to your inquiry and follow up about working together;
  • schedule, confirm, and hold your introductory call;
  • keep a record of the conversation so we can serve you properly.

We do not sell your data or share it for marketing purposes.

3. Third-party processors

We rely on a small number of trusted services to receive and store what you send. They process your data on our behalf, each under their own privacy terms:

  • Google — the contact form is delivered through Google Apps Script and stored in Google Sheets.
  • Telegram — we receive a notification message when a new inquiry arrives, so we can reply quickly.
  • Cal.com — powers the booking scheduler and stores the details of any call you book.

These providers may process and store data on infrastructure outside the Republic of Moldova.

4. Data retention

We keep your inquiry and booking details for as long as needed to respond to you and, if we begin working together, for the duration of that relationship plus any period required for our legitimate business or legal records. If no engagement follows, we remove inquiry data once it is no longer useful. You can ask us to delete your data at any time (see below).

5. Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal data you have shared with us, and to stop contacting you. To make a request, email hello@splendessa.com from the address you provided, and we will respond within a reasonable time. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable data-protection law.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our tools or practices change. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email us at hello@splendessa.com.
